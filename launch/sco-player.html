Stepping through this learning content

The Next  button takes you to each section of this learning content. Some sections may require you to interact with its content before you can continue—for example, it will ask you to click an image or follow a link. In such case, the content page will give you the instruction to follow. When you complete all instructions, then you may continue.

The Back  button takes you back to the previous section.

When in a quiz, these buttons will take you to the next or previous question.

Knowing where you are

The pair of numbers on the left of the Back button indicate: (1) the current section you are in, and (2) the total number of sections in this learning content.

When in a quiz however, these will tell you: (1) the current question number you are in, and (2) how many questions there are in the quiz.

Finding content within this lesson

Use the Contents Menu  button to show or hide the Table of Contents for this learning content.

After you complete this help guide, you can try and bring up the Table of Contents and click an entry in it. It should take you to that section. A section will have a tick [] next to it if you have completed it. Otherwise, it will have a dot [].
