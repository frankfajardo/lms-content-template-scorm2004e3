Incident Reporting

All injuries and incidents (including near misses) must be reported within 24 hours.

No matter how minor the incident, it must be reported. Incidents are to be reported to your manager via an online form which will automatically be registered with your HSE Manager. This form is also used to report any pain or discomfort experienced when performing work tasks.

In some occasions an investigation or assessment may be required to ensure the causes are determined and the incident can be prevented from happening again.
