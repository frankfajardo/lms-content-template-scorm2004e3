Looking After Yourself

Working in an office environment requires you to understand office ergonomics. You can easily create an ergonomically friendly workstation by following the guidelines in the workstation checklist. The main aim is to position yourself to avoid awkward and static postures.

Our Global Health and Safety Policy and standards requires all employees to complete the workstation checklist when you join Pearson and annually thereafter. Creating healthy office postural habits is essential to looking after yourself.
