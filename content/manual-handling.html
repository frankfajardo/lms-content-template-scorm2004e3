Manual Handling and Lifting Safety

Any activity that requires a person to exert force to lift, lower, push, pull, carry or otherwise move, hold, and/or restrain any object is considered manual handling. Unsafe manual handling may cause a variety of injuries and conditions.

Unsafe manual handling may cause a variety of injuries and conditions. Every time you lift or move an object you should remember some important safety steps.
