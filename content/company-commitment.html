Pearson Australia's Commitment to Workplace Safety

We are committed to preventing illness and injury by taking all reasonable steps to provide a safe and healthy work environment. While every precaution is taken to promote safety in and around the offices, you must take personal responsibility in maintaining a safe work environment and work practices.

Safety is everyone's responsibility, including that of visitors and contractors. To ensure the safety of all, visitors and contractors will be required to sign the Visitor's Book and wear a Visitor's badge at all times.

Pearson Australia's Health and Safety Management System complies with the Group Health and Safety minimum standards.

We recommend that you bookmark our Health, Safety and Environment site in Neo.
