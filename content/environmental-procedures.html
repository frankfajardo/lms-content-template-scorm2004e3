Environmental Standard Operating Procedures

Action in the workplace

We all retain a degree of responsibility for ensuring protection of environmental values and meeting our environmental targets. In order to discharge our legal obligations under the environmental management system, employees must take all reasonable steps to protect the environment by:

Waste Management: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Use the recycling bins provided.
  • Only print documents when absolutely necessary.

Energy: Switch Off!

  • Turn off all electrical equipment when not in use and/or at the end of the day.
  • When leaving a room switch off lights where possible.

Water

  • Use the dishwasher efficiently and do not leave taps running.

Travel/Transport

  • Use video-conferencing for meetings rather than travelling to other locations.
