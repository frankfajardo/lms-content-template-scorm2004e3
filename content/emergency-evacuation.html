Emergency Evacuation Procedure

Ensure you familiarise yourself with your sites evacuation procedures.

When you hear the Alert Tone: "Beep Beep"

  1. Stop all work and be alert.
  2. Wait for Warden instructions.
  3. Remain with your visitors at all times.

When you hear the Evacuation Tone: "Woop Woop"

In some sites you may also hear an announcement to evacuate the building.
  1. Remain calm.
  2. Assemble at the nearest exit. Do not waste time by packing up your belongings. Do not take coffee, food or any other items with you.
  3. Remain with your visitors at all times.
  4. Evacuate the building under direction of the Wardens. If no Warden presents at this time, move to step 5.
  5. Walk carefully, quickly and hold handrails.
  6. Proceed to the nearest assembly area and locate your manager and team.
  7. Remain at the assembly point until the safety check is complete or advised by the wardens.
  8. Do not smoke at the assembly point.
  9. Do not go to your car or leave the site.
