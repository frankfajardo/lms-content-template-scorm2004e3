Your Responsibility to Workplace Safety

Your everyday actions require basic safety rules. When moving within your worksite you must follow these basic safety rules:

  • Hold onto handrails at all times when walking up and down stairs.
  • No climbing on or over obstacles.
  • Remove any slip or trip hazards immediately as you see them.
  • Keep walkways and exits clear at all times.
  • Read and comply with all safety signs.
  • Do not run.
